## 📚 Research

**"Attention-Based Rewards in LSTM for Detection of Stimming Behaviour in Autistic Children"**
*Egyptian Informatics Journal · Elsevier · Published November 2025*
🔗 [View on ScienceDirect](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S1110866525002154)

**Authors:** J. Christina, J. Dhalia Sweetlin, **D. Rahul Prasanth**

**Abstract:**
Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurological condition characterized by challenges in social communication and repetitive stimming behaviours such as arm flapping, head banging, and spinning. This study proposes a novel **attention-based reward-driven LSTM** architecture for detecting stimming behaviours from video data. The LSTM captures long-term temporal dependencies across frames, while the attention mechanism highlights the most relevant features — adapting to the diverse and individualized nature of ASD. This reward-based deep learning approach differentiates stimming behaviours between children with ASD and typically developing children, demonstrating significant improvement in action detection accuracy.

**Keywords:** `Autism Spectrum Disorder` `LSTM` `Attention Mechanism` `Stimming Detection` `Deep Learning` `Computer Vision` `Healthcare AI`
